Personal Loan Details

A personal loan is a type of unsecured loan that can be used for various purposes such as medical expenses, debt consolidation, or home improvement.

  • Loan amount: $5,000 - $50,000
  • Loan term: 1 - 7 years
  • Interest rate: 6% - 24% (depending on credit score)
  • Repayment frequency: Monthly

Personal loans are typically unsecured, meaning they do not require collateral. The loan approval depends on your credit score and income level.
